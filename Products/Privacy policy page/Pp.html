California Online Privacy Policy Act

California Online Privacy Policy Act (CalOPPA) requires that any and all information that you collect about california consumers be listed with your privacy policy. The effective date of the CalOPPA is 1st january, 2014.




General Data Protection Regulation

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) protects EU individual's fundamental right and freedom particularly their right to protection of their personal data. Effective date of the GDPR is 28th May, 2018.




California Consumer Privacy Act

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is a state wide privacy law that regulates how businesses all over the world are allowed to handle the Personal Information(PI) of california residents. The effective date of CCPA is 1st january, 2020.
